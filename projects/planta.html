Projects

planta is an interactive installation that allows viewers to manipulate sound and projections through plants. The idea behind it is to create a playful synergy between nature and technology. Each plant is connected to a specific sound and image output that can be activated by touching the leaves. The intensity of these effects increases with the strength of the touch. The technology behind it is composed of an single-board microcontroller, a processing sketch, an Ableton set, lots of cables and a controllable LED strip.
