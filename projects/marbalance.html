Marbalance is an interactive game in which the player has to avoid red dots. The player, a blue dot, is controlled with the help of a balancing board. The board is equipped with a sensor that records the movement of the board and transmits it to a computer. In the game, you can choose between four difficulty levels, which influence the number and speed of the red dots. From 22.06.2024 - 09.03.2025, the game will be on display at the Technoseum Mannheim.
