Projects

All photographs by Anna Thiel

Captcha Design Festival is organized by students of the University of Applied Sciences Mannheim. It was taking place for the 8th time from 29. August – 09. September 2022. In three different workshops the teachers and participants came together for one week to connect, learn new techniques and work on creative projects. After this week the results were exhibited at Kunstverein Mannheim. The topic for 2022 was »symbiosis – a cohabitation of analogue and digital«. We were very happy to welcome Cihan Tamti, Guido Schmidt and Carolin Wanitzek as workshop leaders.

The opening of the festival was the symposium at the youth centre Forum in Mannheim. We invited Ann Richter, Andreas Uebele and Julian Zimmermann as guest speakers, who gave our audience inspiring talks. The workshops were the main part of the Captcha Design Festival. Lead by our workshop leaders, every participant invented the meaning of »symbiosis« new. After three days of hard work, everything created in the workshops was exhibited at the Mannheimer Kunstverein. We held a Vernissage to open up the exhibition.
