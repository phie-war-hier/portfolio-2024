The TECHNOarena is a show format of the TECHNOseum Mannheim on the subject of future technologies. School classes attend talks on the latest technological innovations given by professionals from research institutes and companies. Afterwards, the students can become active themselves and take part in a workshop.

How will the world look in the future? Everyone can help design the future with science and research and yet we don't know what our world will look like in 10 years' time. The design concept plays with the uncertainty of what will happen in the future. The future is a space that is open, wide and impenetrable. The concept incorporates minimalist elements such as gradients and geometric figures to reflect the atmosphere of the future. The outlines of technologies are illustrated, which change halfway through into an abstract, undefinable form and therefore represent the development into the unknown and intangible. The abstract form gives everyone the freedom to fantasize about what our future could look like with new technologies.
