Since not every visitor has technical knowledge, we have aimed to abstract SAP BTP and make it understandable for everyone. Instead of technical app components, visitors take individual rocket parts such as the engine, tank and cockpit and assemble them into a functioning rocket. The rocket can then be launched like an app and is then hosted on SAP BTP. Using the QR code that is displayed after the launch, visitors can open the app on their own smartphone and try out the app's possibilities.
